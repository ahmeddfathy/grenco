Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your response time?

+

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent technical support, we offer 24/7 assistance.

Do you offer free consultations?

+

Yes! We offer free initial consultations to assess your recycling needs and recommend the best solution for your business.

What are your business hours?

+

Our main office is open Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We also offer 24/7 technical support.

Can I schedule a site visit?

+

Absolutely! We can arrange a site visit to demonstrate our machines and discuss your specific needs. Please contact our sales team.

Do you provide training?

+

Yes, comprehensive training is included with every machine installation. We also offer ongoing training and certification programs.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and can arrange financing options. Contact our sales team for payment plan details.
