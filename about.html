About Grenco

Leading the sustainable future through innovative recycling technology

Our Story

Grenco was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize plastic recycling through smart technology. What started as a small initiative to address the growing plastic waste problem has evolved into a leading provider of innovative recycling solutions for businesses worldwide.

Our journey began when our founders recognized that traditional recycling methods were inefficient and costly. They set out to create machines that not only recycle plastic more effectively but also provide real-time data and insights to help businesses optimize their operations.

Today, Grenco machines are deployed in over 75 facilities across 10 countries, helping businesses reduce waste, cut costs, and contribute to a more sustainable planet.
